## Style Prompt

Municipal operations control center with an eco-civic identity: clean light surfaces, deep green institutional accents, amber urgency markers, and practical dashboard density. The video should feel calm, capable, and field-ready rather than flashy: city services, daily work orders, reviews, vehicles, HR, and mobile reporting moving through one connected system.

## Colors

- Page background: #f4faf2
- Ink: #112019
- Deep green: #1f6d34
- Action green: #2f7f46
- Fresh green: #7ab785
- Amber signal: #d4aa38
- Soft panel: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.90)

## Typography

- Display: Exo 2, 800 for titles and main numerals
- Body: IBM Plex Sans, 400-600 for Mongolian copy
- Data: IBM Plex Mono, 500 for counters and status chips

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use neon, purple-blue gradients, or dark tech styling.
- Do not use oversized marketing cards that hide the actual app screens.
- Do not use generic stock imagery; show real app screenshots and civic workflow cues.
- Do not use rounded pill-heavy decoration outside real status chips.
